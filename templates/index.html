Our Story

We serve thousands of people!

Zimbabwe Association of Pension Funds is home to thousands of individuals who get expert advice from our consultants. The promulgation of the Pension and Provident Funds Act in 1976 spurred Mr. J.S. Robins of Rio Tinto to call for a meeting to which he invited all interested parties composed mainly of self administered pension funds, to seek recognition by Government as the sole and authentic body representing the Pension Funds Industry.
